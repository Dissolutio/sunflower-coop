Membership Process

Sunflower members tend to stay for several years, so vacancies can be rare, but when there is an opening, we send out an open call for applicants. This involves posting to our website and Facebook, sending out an email to our vacancy mailing list, as well as advertising in other places like the Austin Co-op Facebook group, Craigslist, and the south Wheatsville bulletin board. We don’t keep a standing waitlist, so all applicants have equal footing.

Potential members will submit an application and interview with all current house members. Once several applicants have completed this process, the house will vote to determine the new member based on concensus voting.
