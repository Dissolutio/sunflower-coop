History

Sunflower is one of the oldest, continuously operating, independent co-ops in Austin (Whitehall Co-op being the oldest). It was founded in September of 1980, “by a group of four men and women living in Prahna House, who envisioned establishing a small, vegetarian, independent co-operative.”

Over the past __ years, hundreds of Austinites have called Sunflower home. The care and contribution of those members allowed Sunflower to grow into the mature and stable co-op it is today.

Decades worth of documents, photos, notes, and __ have been stored away and archived. In 2017, an initiative to digitize, preserve, and publicly display these archives began. Co-op member Laura Aavang led the charge by scanning these “Sunflower artifacts” and creating a website to house them.

It is our hope that past, present, and future members may enjoy browsing – and reminiscing over – these special memories. Moreover, we enthusiastically welcome any members who wish to aid our record-keeping by sharing their Sunflower experiences / knowledge. There are many ways to help!
